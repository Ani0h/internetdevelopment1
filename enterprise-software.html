(CRM) Customer Relationships Management

This is software that helps businesses connect with their past, present and future customers. it uses technology to hold information on its customers in regards to different aspect of the business such as sales, marketing, customer services and technical support.

It is responsible for automated communications between the customer and the business, in a sense bonding the two, which inspires customer satisfaction and loyalty. The CRM manages this by gathering information about the customers across all platforms and their opinions on the business

.

(CMS) Content Management Systems

Software programme that is used by businesses to manage, create or modify digital content for use in regards to their online presence. The users do not need to be versed in HTML or have any coding skills or background to use this software.

A content management system is made up of two parts, A content management application (CMA) and A content delivery application (CDA) the CMA allows the user to add and manage content while the CDA makes it visible to the website visitors.
